Certificate Details

We are excited to award you a certificate once your project is approved. Your certificate will be mailed to you after your project is approved.

  1. Submit Your Project:
    Once you're done, submit your project through the platform. Make sure you follow the submission instructions carefully to avoid any problems.
  2. Approval Process:
    After you submit, our team will review your project. You will receive an email once your project is approved.
  3. Certificate Issuance:
    Once approved, you’ll get your certificate in your inbox! It will include your name, project title, and completion date.
  4. Certificate Format:
    The certificate will be in PDF format, so you can easily download, print, or share it as needed.

If you need any help along the way or have any questions, don’t hesitate to reach out to us. We’re here to support you!
